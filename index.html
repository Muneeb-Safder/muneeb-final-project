GOKU

Goku is a fictional character and the protagonist of the popular manga and anime series Dragon Ball created by Akira Toriyama. He is a Saiyan warrior originally named Kakarot, who was sent to Earth as a baby but later grows up to become one of the greatest martial artists in the universe. Goku is known for his incredible strength, determination, and his relentless pursuit of becoming stronger through intense training and battles. He often defends Earth from various villains and threats alongside his friends and fellow Z Fighters.


VEGITA

Vegeta is another prominent character from the Dragon Ball series, created by Akira Toriyama. He is a Saiyan prince and the rival of Goku. Like Goku, Vegeta is a powerful warrior with an intense desire to become the strongest fighter in the universe. He initially serves as an antagonist, but over time, he undergoes significant character development and ultimately becomes a key ally to Goku and the other Z Fighters in their battles against powerful foes. Vegeta is known for his pride, arrogance, and determination to surpass Goku, as well as his complex personality and moral struggles.


GOHAN

Gohan is a central character in the Dragon Ball series, and he is the eldest son of Goku and Chi-Chi. Like his father, Gohan is a Saiyan-human hybrid, which grants him immense potential for strength and fighting ability. Initially introduced as a young child, Gohan's character undergoes significant development throughout the series. He is portrayed as a kind-hearted and intelligent individual who often struggles with the expectations placed upon him by his Saiyan heritage and his mother's desire for him to focus on academics rather than martial arts. Despite this, Gohan proves himself to be a formidable warrior, often stepping up to protect Earth and his loved ones in times of crisis. He plays a pivotal role in many of the series' major battles, and his strength and potential are key factors in the ongoing struggle against powerful adversaries. Throughout the series, Gohan's character evolves from a shy and timid boy into a confident and powerful fighter, ultimately becoming one of Earth's greatest defenders.


TRUNKS

Trunks is another significant character in the Dragon Ball series. There are two versions of Trunks: Future Trunks and Present Trunks. Future Trunks: He is the son of Vegeta and Bulma from an alternate future timeline where the Earth is ravaged by the androids and later by the villainous Cell. Future Trunks travels back in time to warn the Z Fighters about the impending threat of the androids and to seek their help in preventing the devastation of his timeline. He is characterized by his serious demeanor, sense of responsibility, and strong sense of justice. Present Trunks: This version of Trunks is the son of Vegeta and Bulma from the main timeline of the Dragon Ball series. He is introduced as a young child and is a close friend of Goten, Goku's second son. Present Trunks shares many similarities with his future counterpart but grows up in a more peaceful era without the threat of androids. As he grows older, he becomes a skilled fighter and plays a significant role in various story arcs, including battling powerful enemies alongside the other Z Fighters. Both versions of Trunks are known for their distinctive appearance, which includes spiky lavender hair and a signature sword. They are both strong and capable warriors who contribute significantly to the ongoing battles to protect Earth and its inhabitants.


BROLLY

Broly is a legendary character in the Dragon Ball series, known for his immense strength and iconic appearance. Initially introduced as an antagonist in the movie "Dragon Ball Z: Broly – The Legendary Super Saiyan," Broly quickly became a fan favorite due to his raw power and unique backstory. Born with an exceptionally high power level, Broly is a Saiyan warrior with unparalleled strength, earning him the title of the "Legendary Super Saiyan." His massive physique, wild green hair, and intense demeanor make him a formidable opponent in battle. Broly's original backstory involves being exiled to a desolate planet by King Vegeta due to his uncontrollable power. Later interpretations of the character expand on his origins and motivations, adding depth to his character. Throughout the series, Broly engages in epic battles against Goku, Vegeta, and other powerful foes, showcasing his overwhelming strength and ferocity. Despite his initially antagonistic role, Broly has become a beloved and iconic figure in the Dragon Ball universe, leaving a lasting impact on fans worldwide.
